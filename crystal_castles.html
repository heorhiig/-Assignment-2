Crystal Castles
Album: (II) (2010)

"(II)" is the second studio album by electronic duo Crystal Castles, released in 2010. Known for its ethereal soundscapes, distorted vocals, and a balance between chaos and beauty, this album cemented Crystal Castles' position as pioneers in the experimental electronic music scene.

Themes of alienation, rebellion, and emotion are deeply woven into the fabric of "(II)", making it a timeless masterpiece.
