Arctic Monkeys
Album: AM (2013)

"AM" is the fifth studio album by the English rock band Arctic Monkeys. Released in 2013, it features some of their biggest hits and showcases a mix of rock, indie, and R&B influences. The album was highly acclaimed and includes iconic tracks like *"Do I Wanna Know?"* and *"R U Mine?"*.

Arctic Monkeys is one of the most influential modern rock bands, known for their unique sound and powerful lyrics. "AM" remains a fan favorite for its catchy tunes and sophisticated production.
